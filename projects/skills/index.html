A Pale Blue Dot

Acquiring the skills to develop software takes a lot of time and effort, which sometimes means losing a bit of humility. That makes the qualification levels difficult to understand, there is no standard test that determines if you are a junior, mid or senior developer. So you can measure my skills by checking that most of our projects are at the top of the search engine results, are Gtmetrix grade A and have a pretty nice interface design.

For those people who sometimes lose their sense of reality, I leave these photos, behind me is the most powerful democracy humanity has seen, which is impressive, however, it exists on a small planet that from a distance looks like a pale blue dot

"It has been said that astronomy is a humbling and character-building experience. There is perhaps no better demonstration of the folly of human conceits than this distant image of our tiny world. To me, it underscores our responsibility to deal more kindly with one another, and to preserve and cherish the pale blue dot, the only home we've ever known..." Carl Sagan
